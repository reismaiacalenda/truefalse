1. GENERAL INFORMATION

1.1.  Types of Users:  Calenda Users are Collaborators, Administrators and Guests, as defined in the Terms of Use.

1.2.  Forms of Data Processing:  Reis Maia's way of processing personal data will vary according to its performance. Calenda has some features, including:

1.2.1.  Employee agenda:  Management of the calendar of meetings and organizational events in which the employee is a participant. Reis Maia will be fully aware of the participants involved, allowing employees to schedule, confirm and cancel their attendance.

1.2.2.  Resource and service management:  Session aimed at managing stocks of assets and services that may be provided internally to meet the needs of the corporation.

1.2.3.  Reserveable space management:  Control of the availability of allocable spaces. These spaces are reserved by Users, who register their participation in their calendars.
